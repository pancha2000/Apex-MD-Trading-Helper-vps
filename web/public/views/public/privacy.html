Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Apex-MD ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our crypto trading platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and hashed password when you register.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, scan history, and trade records within the platform.
  • API Keys: Exchange API keys you optionally add are stored encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption. We never store keys in plaintext.
  • WhatsApp Number: If you choose to link WhatsApp, we store your WhatsApp JID to deliver notifications.
  • AI API Keys: GROQ and Gemini API keys you optionally provide are stored encrypted.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the trading platform services.
  • To deliver WhatsApp notifications and trading signals you have opted into.
  • To improve platform features and user experience.
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud.
  • To communicate important service updates.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for all API keys and sensitive credentials.
  • Scrypt hashing for passwords — never stored in plaintext.
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised systems only.

4. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Binance API: For real-time market data and price feeds (read-only).
  • Groq API / Google Gemini: For AI-powered analysis summaries (only when you provide your own key).
  • WhatsApp (via Baileys): For optional notification delivery.
  • Alternative.me: For Fear & Greed Index data.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies

We use a single session cookie (apex_user_session) to keep you logged in. This cookie is required for the platform to function and contains no personally identifiable information beyond your session token. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Closed trade records are automatically deleted after 7 days. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Remove linked API keys and WhatsApp connections at any time through Settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting a notice on the platform.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through the platform's support channels.
